Ardrey Kell TSA Projects

Digital Video Production 2012 - 2013

The Digital Video Production team pulled out all of the stops for this one. It's no surprise it won 1st place at both the Regional level and the State level.

Digital Video Production 2013 - 2014

The Digital Video Production team tried their best to produce a documentary about the Video Game Design project Lunar Lander. Despite not winning a single award it was an amazing job filmed by Matthew Halsted and edited by Sharvil Desai.

Video Game Design 2013 - 2014

Here you can download Lunar Lander. Lunar Lander was the AKTSA Video Game project for the 2013 - 2014 year. It won 3rd at Regionals and 3rd at States.
http://lunarlander.dmitrypustovit.com/

Open Source Software Development 2013 - 2014

Here you can download the 2013 - 2014 Open Source Software Development project. It got into the semi-finals at Nationals, however did not finish in the top ten. The program was created by Dmitry Pustovit.
https://github.com/DmitryPustovit/AutoBack

Webmasters 2013 - 2014

The Webmasters team including Vishnu Menon, Dmitry Pustovit, and Abhijith Tammanagari created an amazing website teaching the basics of web development. The amazing site won first at the State level. Sadly it did not place at the National level.
http://vishnumenon.com/webmaster/index.html#/
