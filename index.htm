About Mystic Static

Mystic Static was founded in February 2013 by Dmitry Pustovit. Its original purpose was to give storys a physical medium, in this case video games. Through it brief existence it has gone through many different changes and ideas. Our goal is simple, create video games that we think are interesting, we would want to play, or we think would be fun to make.
