My name is Dmitry Pustovit. I am a developer and a designer. I started programming when I started high school. I wanted to create programs and websites from scratch. It seemed like the ultimate challenge, to make something that was completely mine. While I began learning how to program, I started it learn the basics of design. Over time my skills as a designer increased but, I am still learning. I believe that is essential for any designer and developer to always be learning. The minds of people are always changing and new technology is always being developed. It is essential for a developers and designers to always keep up with the newest trends and the newest technology in order to keep their work up to date. Needless to say this website was created completely from scratch. I hope you enjoy.
