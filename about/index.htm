About Mystic Static
Mystic Static was founded in February 2013 by Dmitry Pustovit.
Mystic Static is an Independent or Indie Game Studio that is based out of Charlotte, North Carolina.
Our HQ
Come have a cup of coffee, or tea if you prefer.
The Story
In 2013 a group of friends decided to make games. It was founded in Charlotte, North Carolina.

Mystic Static was founded with a simple goal, to create worlds, fill them with characters, and tell magnificent stories. In order to do this our goal is to create video games that we think are interesting and fun, that we ourselves would like to play, or games that we simply believe would be fun to make.
Today
Today only a few of those friends, who came together to create Mystic Static, remain a part of Mystic Static.

We try to stay true to the goal that Mystic Static was founded with by attempting to create a truly unique experience when someone plays one of our games.
Current Projects
We are always working of a variety of projects. Some of these projects never get past the concept stage.

However, if they reach alpha stage we begin to post concept art and early game play footage to keep everyone aware of our progress.
